Welcome to Piccoloinc.com

Transportation and logistics business is one of the largest and most complex industries. Market imperfections, structural shifts and external developments, like industry deregulation, rising energy cost and driver shortages present additional and costly challenges. Major transportation companies are demanding large rate increases, while companies have been unable to pass the rising transportation costs to consumers. In the past, supply chain management used to be exclusively about low pricing and speed. In the networked economy, the major supply chain imperative will be collaboration and management of increasingly complex transportation networks and improvement of operational excellence.

We provide innovative transportation management solutions that optimize the entire supply chain and make it more agile and adaptable.
