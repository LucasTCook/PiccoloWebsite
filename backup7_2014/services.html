Supply Chain Challenges. Supply Chain Solutions.

Creating competitive advantages through operational excellence and ultra-responsive supply chain management.

World class companies have built supply chains that are not only cost efficient and fast, but also agile, adaptable and aligned. Agile supply chains respond quickly to short-term fluctuations in supply and demand, adaptable supply chains adjust quickly to external shifts, and aligned supply chains provide incentive for improved performance throughout the transportation process. Piccolo Inc. provides a number of integrated services offering to help your company build and manage an supply chain that is cost-effective, fast, agile, adaptable and aligned. Our transportation management solutions include:

* Third-Party Transportation Management
* Express Freight Management
* Professional and Financial Services

Third-Party Transportation Management:

Our asset-lite business model allows us to respond quickly to short-term fluctuations in supply and demand, maintain a flexible transportation environment and align the goals of our clients with that of carriers, while helping clients to reduce transportation expenses by 10% - 30%. Our services include:

* Analysis of current transportation strategy
* Bottleneck and problem analysis
* Formulation of optimal transportation strategy
* Carrier selection
* Transport operation management
* Confirmation of pick-up and delivery
* Freight audit and payment service

Express Freight Management:

Piccolo Express Freight Management provides time-critical and reliable delivery of LTL and FTL at competitive rates. To schedule an Express Freight shipment, please email: express@piccoloinc.com or call 270-781-6021.

Professional and Financial Services:

Piccolo Inc. delivers value and helps clients to optimize their supply chain through consultation, problem analysis, objective setting, transportation strategy formulation, operational implementation and feedback analysis. Our financial services help clients to improve working capital management, operational cash flow and company value. Our services include:

* Design of an ultra-responsive supply chain
* Carrier selection and management of operation
* Freight audit and payment service
* Transportation expense invoicing and financial analysis

Warehousing and Distribution:

Piccolo Inc. provides distribution and warehousing space at highly competitive rates and has the capabilities to manage the warehousing facilities. To receive more information about our warehousing services, please email: warehousing@piccoloinc.com
